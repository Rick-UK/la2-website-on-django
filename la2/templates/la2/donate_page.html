Support for Your Lineage 2 Server

Choose a convenient method to make a voluntary contribution to support the development of the Lineage 2 server, and receive Coins as a token of our gratitude.

  • 1. By Bank Card Online: Make a Contribution
  • 2. By Sending an SMS: Send SMS

Where Do the Contributions Go?

Our goal is to create the best Lineage 2 game world in collaboration with our players.

All voluntary contributions are directed towards:

  • 1. Server development
  • 2. Payment for services of third-party organizations and individuals involved in server maintenance and functionality


Additionally, these contributions help form a "Reward Fund".

Funds from this pool will be returned to players as rewards for their active participation in developing the Lineage 2 game world.

Please note: The Lineage 2 server is a non-commercial project. By supporting the server, you are supporting yourself!
